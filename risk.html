  1. Get the latest updates from your mobile manufacturer

    In most of the situation, the newly discovered bugs or loopholes can be modified by the latest software update. Therefore, always updating the software to date is important for E-privacy.


  2. Install anti-virus program

    2


  3. Do not download apps from unknown source

    Users can download apps from google play store and apple store. However, some of the apps can not be downloaded in these store. This may due to the app is inappropriate, contain virus,etc. Some of the user may download it from other source. Also, some of them may be free in other source but not free in google play and apple store. However, downloading apps from source other than google play store and apple store increase the chances of infected by virus. Also, it may create loophole for hackers to steal users information.


  4. Check the authorization required before downloading apps

    When users download apps from google play store or apple store, they need to agree the app to access some authority such as Photos/Media/Files,etc. Users need to press the accept button in order to download and use that app. Accepting the app to access your Photos/Media/Files means that the app can store, modify your photos, media and files. Therefore, users should think whether the app requires the authorization to work or the app is just trying to steal your information.


  5. Stay alert to abnormal phone situation and reset phone if needed

    Abnormal phone situation such as rapid reduce of speed and batteries may indicated some apps are running. This apps may contains virus and hacker is stealing information from your phone. Hence, users should consider resetting the phone if above situation occurs in their phone.


  6. Do not connect public wi-fi (unsecured wi-fi) if possible

    Try to avoid using unknown, unsecured wi-fi. Some hackers may set-up some phishing wi-fi hotspots that the SSID is same as what user expected. For example, the name of the shopping mall, restaurant. If users connected their mobile phone into these phishing wifi, their information and their internet activities may be stolen by hackers.
    Also, some unsecured wi-fi may be set-up by some shop or restaurant so as to provide free wi-fi for their customers. However, the security level of these wi-fi may be low. Hackers may hack the wi-fi and steal the information from other users connecting that wi-fi.
    Therefore, users should avoid using unknown or unsecure wi-fi. Wi-fi provided from your telecommunication service provider is a good example of trusted wi-fi.

Reason for using Questionnarie

1-Consideration for respondents
The format of questionnaires is close-ended. Therefore, it is easier for respondents to answer and lower the risk of respondents losing patience.


2-Consideration for group members
Close-ended questionnaires are also easier for analyze. As there are less irrelevant, complicated and confused answer (open-ended questions) received from respondents, group members are able to analyze and compare the results efficiently.


3-Consideration for results’ reliability
Questionnaires reduce bias and intrusion when comparing to face-to-face interviews and group interviews. Questions in questionnaires avoid leading questions and thus, reduce bias as to ensure reliability of the result. Moreover, there is no research data or opinions from other respondents to interrupt one respondents from answering the questionnaire. Therefore, results are more reliable.


Target Group

Invited respondents are university students and also, smartphone users aged 18-23 in order to find out their experiences in using smartphones and their awareness of the privacy risk and security associated with using smartphones.


Distribution

Paper-based questionnaires were distributed on the 9th March 2017. To ensure the respondents are solely university students, the above-mentioned method was adopted. For online surveys, no doubt, it is relatively cost-effective and time-effective, however, unwanted responses from other groups of people may be received. This may cause time to separate the unwanted responses.
